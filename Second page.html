Quiz Rules and walkthrough

Welcome to the Smart quiz

This is a multiple-choice quiz platform where you can test your knowledge in various categories. We offer three sets of quizzes: Technical Quiz, Non-Technical Quiz, and Cricket Quiz. Each quiz has its own set of rules, but the overall gameplay remains similar.

General Rules:

1.Number of Questions: Each quiz will consist of 10 multiple-choice questions.
2.Time Limit: There is no time limit for any of the quizzes. You can take your time and answer at your own pace.
3.Scoring: You will receive 1 point for each correctly answered question. No points will be deducted for incorrect answers.
4.Reviewing Answers: After submitting your answers for a quiz, you can review the correct answers and explanations (if provided) for all questions.
5.Retaking Quizzes: You can retake any quiz as many times as you like. Your score will be updated based on your latest attempt.

Walkthrough

1.Select a Quiz: Choose the quiz you'd like to take from the available options: Technical Quiz, Non-Technical Quiz, or General Knowledge Quiz.
2.Read the Question: Carefully read the question presented on the screen.
3.Review Answer Options: Each question will have four possible answer choices. Read through all options before making your selection.
4.Select Your Answer: Click the radio button next to the answer you believe is correct. You can only select one answer per question.
5.Submit Answer: Once you've chosen your answer, click the "Submit Answer" button.
6.Next Question or Results:If you haven't answered all the questions, the next question will be displayed automatically. If you've answered all the questions, your score will be displayed along with options to review answers or retake the quiz.
